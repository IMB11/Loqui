Simple.

Loqui uses a simple and effective process to provide as many translations as possible for as many mods as possible.

The Process

Loqui works seamlessly in the background, ensuring your favorite mods are instantly translated.

Indexing Stage
Loqui traverses your mods folder, gathering information on mods and what en_us.json language files they may contain.
Downloading Stage
Using the information it gathered about your mods, Loqui automatically downloads any available translations from it's API via Lokalise.
Uploading Stage
At the same time, Loqui will upload any new en_us.json files retrieved from the indexing process to it's API, allowing community translators to quickly translate these files.

Effective.

Loqui is used by 1000s of players and in various high-ranking modpacks - giving it the large-userbase it needs to target as many mods as possible.
